About me

I am a First-Year Graduate Student Pursuing Masters in Robotics Engineering at Worcester Polytechnic Institute, Massachusetts. I completed my B.E in Electronics Engineering from the Dwarkadas J. Sanghvi College of Engineering, Mumbai University. My research interests lie broadly in the fields of Controls, Deep Learning and Path Planning for solving real-world problems.

After completion of my undergrad, I seized the opportunity to work with Earth Energy EV as an R&D Intern where I designed and manufactured wiring harness and electronic control unit for an electric motorbike. Tracking my performance and marking my immense dedication and hard work, I was offered a full-time position as Electronic Design Engineer where, I was leading Signal Processing and Control Algorithm Team. We have designed a Level-2 Electric Smart Charger with a new Li-key (light key) technology. Also worked on an autonomous electric delivery robot. This projects turned out to be the vast reservoir of learning and gaining the hands-on practical on software like Atmel Studios, SolidWorks, Rapid Harness, LTspice and software libraries OpenCV and Scikit-Learn, etc.

Balancing my regular academics training in my undergrad, I was an active member of Team DJS Racing for two years. DJS Racing is a Formula Student team of D.J.Sanghvi College of Engineering taking part in Formula Bharat, Formula Student Germany and Formula Student Austria. As an Electronics and Data Acquisition Lead of the team, developed an industrial grade Electrical Wiring Loom under guidance of Spark Minda and effectively developed a closed-loop Drag Reduction System for Aerodynamic package which gets the feedback from Brake Pressure Sensor and Steering Angle position. We further designed a Telemetry system which collects data from various sensors on the car and use that data to improvise its performance of the car.

My association with things on professional work and an extra-curricular front has imbibed in me a deep sense of responsibility and a bigger role to undertake in the future.
